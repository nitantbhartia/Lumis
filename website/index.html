Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 20, 2026

At Lumis ("we", "our"), we respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data Storage
All data regarding your habits, progress, and app selections is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud via CloudKit. We do not sell your data.

2. Screen Time & Family Controls
Lumis uses the Apple Screen Time API (Family Controls) solely to provide the "Shield Apps" feature. This allows you to voluntarily block specific apps during your morning routine.

  • We do not record which apps you install or use.
  • We do not report your usage activity to any external server.
  • The blocking functionality is strictly controlled by your settings within the app.

3. Health Data
If you grant permission, Lumis reads step count and sunlight exposure data from Apple Health to track your progress. This data is processed locally to update your daily stats.

4. Location
We use your location to fetch local weather, sunrise, and sunset times. This location data is not stored permanently on our servers.
